A MODERN, ELEGANT PRIVATE ROOM

Welcome to our nice, clean room biking distance to Facebook, Amazon and Stanford. It’s also just a short drive to Google, Microsoft and other top Silicon Valley companies. It’s a nice, central location in the Bay Area, close to freeway 101, 84 and 880 if you drive and also biking distance to a nearby Caltrain station if you don’t. We are conveniently located close to some shopping near Ikea.

At our home, enjoy a modern and elegant, fully-furnished bedroom, with ample natural morning light for a gradual wake after a restful night's sleep. You can also relax and watch TV in a cozy living room with a comfortable sofa and warm blankets. Alternatively, explore your inner cook in our lovely kitchen or step outside to smell the fresh air while enjoying a quick look at our micro-garden. You can even host friends in the backyard and use our barbecue. Come join us in this peaceful setting.

About us

Hi, it's nice to meet you. My name is Belinda and my boyfriend, Luis, and we are hosts of 2234 Ralmar. To tell you a little about ourselves, I’m currently working at Google on Search, and Luis is working at Google on Youtube. We both graduated from Harvard with degrees in Computer Science, me in ‘17 and him in ‘16, and we’re excited to be part of the Silicon Valley tech scene.

We really like our place, for a variety of reasons, but largely because of the location. The neighborhood seems to be a nice one and it’s also not too far from local tech companies (thankfully!). It’s also really close to some of our favorite restaurants like Fey's and several nearby parks. We've put a lot of loving care into this place, and we hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
